Shhhhh! This is a virtual library.

Sometimes we need some help picking out a book to read. Luckily for you, I have a solution! Choose a category, and see its most popular books (which are all available as audiobooks on Spotify). Happy reading! :)

Data from Spotify and Open Library.

A Spotify Premium subscription is required to access the audiobooks. If Spotify Premium is out of the question for you, try to your local library! Getting a library card gives you access to Libby, an app where you can read or listen to free books and magazines!
